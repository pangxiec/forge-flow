# Workspace Template

Purpose: an operation dashboard for B-side systems.

Use when the PRD mentions home page, dashboard, todo, metrics, alerts, or overview.

Required structure:
- Left or top navigation with 4-7 business modules.
- Top summary area with current business context.
- Metric cards for pending, in progress, abnormal, completed, or similar states.
- Recent records table that can navigate to detail pages.
- A workflow or todo panel showing next actions.

Interaction requirements:
- Metric cards should be clickable.
- Recent records should enter detail.
- Primary action should enter create/edit form.
